## CSS Blend Mode Technique

The tinted logos use a combination of:

- `filter: grayscale(100%) brightness(1.2) contrast(1.1)` - Converts to monochrome, increases brightness and adds contrast
- `mixBlendMode: 'screen'` on the image - Lightens the result
- A colored overlay with `background: var(--accent-primary)` and `mixBlendMode: 'multiply'` - Applies the tint

This preserves the tonal values (light and dark areas) while applying your theme colors with better visibility.
